Features

Everything a student actually needs

No fluff. Just the tools that move the needle on your reading habits.

🔥

Streak Tracking

Daily streaks with smart badges. Miss a day and the AI nudges you back before momentum is lost.

Exam & Deadline Tracking

Add tests, exams, and submission dates. The AI factors these in when recommending how much to read today.

Smart Study Notes

AI Study Schedule

Enter your courses, page counts, and exam dates. ReadSmartly builds a personalised daily plan and redistributes load automatically as you log sessions.

Exam Readiness

Know exactly how prepared you are

ReadSmartly calculates a live exam readiness score based on your progress, reading speed, and time left before each deadline.

📈

Real-Time Readiness Score

Your score updates every time you log a session. It combines pages completed, reading pace, and days remaining into a single percentage indicator.

⚠️

Early Warning Alerts

If your score drops below 60% with less than a week to go, the AI triggers an alert and suggests a catch-up plan before it is too late.

🗓️

Deadline-Driven Targets

Each course gets daily page targets derived from your exam date. Miss a day and targets redistribute automatically to keep you on track.

🔁

Retention Booster

Before starting a new session, the AI surfaces review questions from your previous notes, so you build on what you know rather than re-reading passively.

How it works

Up and running in under 2 minutes

01
Create your account

Sign up with any email address. Name your AI assistant and it will use your name and tone preferences throughout.

02
Tell us about your studies

Choose your study type: undergraduate, postgraduate, or other. Undergrads select their level and courses get auto-populated. Postgrads set up modules or thesis work. You only confirm pages and exam dates.

03
Calibrate your reading speed

Answer a short questionnaire or take a quick reading test. The AI uses this to build a realistic daily plan, with no generic estimates.

04
Study, log, and note

Log sessions by pages read and duration. Add typed notes or record a voice note after each session. Your streak grows automatically.

05
Get smarter over time

The AI learns your patterns, surfaces review questions from past notes, adjusts your schedule as exams approach, and keeps you from falling behind.
